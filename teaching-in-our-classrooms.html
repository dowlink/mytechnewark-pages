Teaching Successfully in Technology-Enabled Classrooms

  • Arrive 10–15 minutes early to power on and test equipment.
  • Test your presentation, audio, and any media before class begins.
  • Connect to RUWireless Secure and ask students to do the same.
  • Prepare materials for both in-person and remote participants.
  • Have a backup plan — a USB drive or cloud-stored copy of your presentation.
  • Call ATS at 973-353-1713 if you need in-class assistance.

Classroom Wireless Success Steps

Steps for faculty and students to ensure a successful experience when using classroom wireless for assessments, polling, and instructional activities.

  • Connect to RUWireless Secure before class
  • Update your OS and browser in advance
  • Close unnecessary apps during assessments
  • Faculty: schedule a practice activity beforehand
  • Have a contingency plan for device issues
