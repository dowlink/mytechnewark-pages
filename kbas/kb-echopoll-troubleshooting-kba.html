EchoPoll Troubleshooting

How To Engage Your Students RU-N & RBS Only EchoPoll Canvas

This article provides solutions to common issues faculty may encounter when using EchoPoll in Canvas. If you continue to experience issues after following the steps below, contact Academic Technology Services (ATS) for assistance.

EchoPoll is licensed exclusively for Rutgers University–Newark and Rutgers Business School faculty. This tool is not available at other Rutgers campuses. Contact ATS at atshelp@newark.rutgers.edu if you have questions about access.

Course Does Not Appear in EchoPoll

Symptoms
  • Your Canvas course is not listed in EchoPoll.
  • EchoPoll displays no available courses.
  • Recently created courses do not appear.
Possible Causes
  • The course has not yet synchronized.
  • The course is unpublished.
  • You are not enrolled as an instructor.
  • The EchoPoll integration has not been activated.
Solutions
  1. 1
    Verify Instructor Enrollment — confirm that you are listed as an Instructor in the Canvas course.
  2. 2
    Publish the Course — some integrations require a published course before synchronization occurs.
  3. 3
    Wait for Synchronization — newly created courses may take time to appear in EchoPoll.
  4. 4
    Relaunch from Canvas — access EchoPoll directly through the Canvas course navigation menu.
If the course still does not appear after completing these steps, contact ATS.
🚫

Students Cannot Access EchoPoll

Symptoms
  • Students report access errors.
  • Students cannot join activities.
  • Students receive permission messages.
Solutions
  1. 1
    Verify Course Enrollment — confirm that students are enrolled in the Canvas course.
  2. 2
    Launch Through Canvas — students should access EchoPoll through Canvas whenever possible.
  3. 3
    Refresh Browser Session — have students complete the following steps:
    1. Close the browser tab.
    2. Return to Canvas.
    3. Relaunch EchoPoll from the course navigation menu.
  4. 4
    Try an Alternate Browser — supported browsers include Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.
⚡

Poll Will Not Launch

Symptoms
  • Activity remains in draft mode.
  • Students cannot respond.
  • Poll does not appear on student screens.
Solutions
  1. 1
    Confirm Activity Status — verify that the activity has been published and launched in EchoPoll.
  2. 2
    Refresh EchoPoll — refresh the browser and relaunch the activity.
  3. 3
    Check Internet Connection — poor connectivity can prevent activities from launching successfully.
  4. 4
    Try a Different Browser — browser extensions or cached data may interfere with EchoPoll functionality.
📋

Attendance Is Not Recording Correctly

Symptoms
  • Students participated but are not listed in the attendance report.
  • Attendance reports appear incomplete.
  • Participation counts are inaccurate.
Possible Causes
  • Students joined late and missed the attendance window.
  • Students did not submit a response.
  • Activity settings were not configured for attendance.
Solutions
  1. 1
    Review Participation Reports — verify that student responses were recorded in EchoPoll.
  2. 2
    Confirm Student Identity — ensure students accessed the activity through the correct Canvas course.
  3. 3
    Use Multiple Participation Opportunities — consider using attendance activities throughout class rather than a single check-in at the start.
  4. 4
    Require Canvas Login — students should access EchoPoll through Canvas and connect using their NetID email address. This ensures their identity is matched correctly in attendance reports.
✏️

Students Cannot Submit Responses

Symptoms
  • Students see the activity but cannot respond.
  • Responses fail to save.
  • Activity appears frozen.
Solutions
  1. 1
    Refresh the Activity — ask students to refresh their browser and try again.
  2. 2
    Verify Internet Connectivity — a weak or interrupted connection can prevent responses from submitting.
  3. 3
    Reopen the Activity — if the session has been closed, students may no longer be able to participate. Reopen the activity in EchoPoll.
  4. 4
    Test Another Device — if available, try another laptop, tablet, or mobile device.
📊

Grades Are Not Syncing to Canvas

Symptoms
  • Scores do not appear in the Canvas Gradebook.
  • Participation grades are missing.
  • Grade synchronization fails or does not complete.
Solutions
  1. 1
    Verify Course Connection — confirm that EchoPoll is connected to the correct Canvas course.
  2. 2
    Review Activity Settings — some activity types may require additional configuration before grades can be synchronized.
  3. 3
    Refresh Grade Synchronization — attempt to run the synchronization process again from within EchoPoll.
  4. 4
    Check Canvas Gradebook — confirm that the corresponding gradebook column exists in Canvas.
  5. 5
    Verify Email Address Match — confirm that the email address associated with the student's EchoPoll account matches the email address in Canvas. A mismatch will prevent grade synchronization.
If grade synchronization problems persist after completing these steps, contact ATS.

  • Enable JavaScript.
  • Allow cookies for Canvas and EchoPoll.
  • Maintain a stable internet connection.
