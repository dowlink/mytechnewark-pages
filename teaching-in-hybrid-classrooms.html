Utilizing a Hybrid Classroom

Follow these best practices to ensure hybrid lectures are ready to present at the beginning of class.

Activating Your Rutgers Zoom Account

Activating your Rutgers Zoom account is paramount to ensure that you are ready to provide hybrid instruction when class begins.

  1. 1
    Visit netid.rutgers.edu
  2. 2
    Select Service Activation
  3. 3
    Log in with your NetID and password
  4. 4
    Select Activate or update a service on my account
  5. 5
    Click Rutgers Zoom
  6. 6
    Click Activate Service
  7. 7
    After activation, sign in at rutgers.zoom.us using SSO
Learn more →

Linking Zoom Meetings Via Canvas

Arrive prepared with a scheduled Zoom meeting on Canvas so students can join remotely when class begins.

  1. 1
    Log on to Canvas at canvas.rutgers.edu
  2. 2
    Open your course
  3. 3
    Select Zoom from the course navigation menu
  4. 4
    Click Schedule a New Meeting
  5. 5
    Enter meeting information — title, date, time, and recurrence settings
  6. 6
    Save the meeting
  7. 7
    Start the meeting when your lecture begins — students join remotely via Zoom
  8. 8
    Recordings can be published to the Canvas course page for students to review later
Learn more →

Hybrid Classroom Best Practices

  • Start class a few minutes early to test technology before instruction begins
  • Launch Zoom at the start of class and confirm settings are correct before admitting participants
  • Monitor Zoom chat regularly for participant questions or statements
  • Repeat in-room questions from students before answering so the microphone clearly captures the question
  • Enable captions when a guest speaker or presenter is connected via Zoom
  • Pause periodically to gauge student understanding and check audio/video quality
  • Share slides, links, and follow-up materials in Canvas after class
